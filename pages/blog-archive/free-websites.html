Why I'm Offering Free Websites...

Building Skills, Relationships, and Trust

Starting a business or launching a new product or service can be both exciting and overwhelming, especially when it comes to establishing an online presence.

For many small business owners and entrepreneurs, creating a website is often the first step in making their brand known to the world. But where do you even begin if you're not tech-savvy or on a tight budget?

That’s where I come in.

I’m currently on my own journey to build my skills in web development, and as part of this, I’m offering free websites to a limited number of small businesses and individuals.

I want to take a moment to explain why I’m doing this, what I have to offer, and how it could benefit both of us.

Building Relationships, Not Just Websites.

First and foremost, my goal is to build genuine relationships. I’m not a large company looking to trap you with hidden fees or a sales pitch.

I’m a growing web developer who’s passionate about helping small businesses and entrepreneurs succeed.

By offering free websites, I aim to learn more about your needs and challenges while honing my skills.

This is a collaborative effort where your feedback helps me grow as a developer, and in return, I can provide you with a functional, attractive online presence at little/no cost.

I believe that creating websites is more than just a technical task; it's about understanding a business's goals, target audience, and unique value proposition.

By working closely with you, I hope to create a site that reflects your brand and meets your specific needs.

Whether you're launching your first website or looking to promote a new product or service, my aim is to offer something genuinely valuable.

What I Offer Right Now

Currently, I’m offering simple one-page landing sites that are perfect for businesses just starting or for promoting a single product or service. These sites are hosted for free, with no hidden costs involved. If you want to add a custom domain, I can guide you through that process, and you can purchase it through my affiliate links or independently—whatever you prefer. I can also help with things like email redirects and setting up on platforms like GitHub Pages or any host of your choice.

While I recognise my skills are still developing, I’m committed to delivering quality work. I have a supportive team of mentors and as I learn and grow, my ability to offer more advanced features will expand. I’ll always be transparent about any costs that may come with additional services.

But for now, what I’m offering is a no-strings-attached, simple, and effective solution to help you get started online.

Why Feedback and Experience Matter to Me

One of the main reasons I’m offering these free services is to gain experience and receive honest feedback. As a new web developer, every project is a chance to learn and improve. Your insights are invaluable in helping me understand what works, what doesn’t, and how I can better serve clients like you in the future.

This experience is not just about building websites; it’s about building trust and demonstrating that I’m a reliable and skilled developer who can deliver on promises. By working with real businesses and real needs, I’m able to learn much more than I ever could from a textbook or a course. It’s a hands-on, practical learning process that benefits both of us.

Let’s Build Something Together

If you’re feeling lost about how to get your business online or need a simple, no-cost way to promote your product or service, I’m here to help. This isn’t just a business transaction; it’s an opportunity for both of us to grow and succeed.

I’m eager to work with people who are as passionate about their ventures. Together, we can create something that not only meets your needs but also sets the stage for our future success.

Let’s connect, discuss your goals, and see how we can bring your vision to life with a website that truly represents your brand. Remember, this is just the beginning of a journey that we can embark on together—let’s make it a positive one!

If you'd like to discuss this, or have any questions, please reach out. I look forward to the possibility of us working together and watching our business' shine online!
